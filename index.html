It started with a dream and a vintage 1960s camper. Joy Local Coffee Co. hit the roads of central Ohio in 2024 as a mobile coffee trailer, bringing expertly crafted drinks directly to community events and local gatherings. Thanks to the incredible support of our neighbors, we expanded our journey in March 2026 by opening a permanent, cozy storefront inside Collective Home Supply. Today, whether you visit our brick-and-mortar shop or catch our rolling "car shop" at a weekend festival, we’re dedicated to pouring joy into every cup.
